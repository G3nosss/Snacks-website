Who We Are

A Legacy of Authentic Indian Flavour

Global Food & Beverages was born from a simple belief: that the rich heritage of Indian namkeen and snacks deserves to be celebrated at every table, in every home, across the nation.

With over 55 meticulously crafted products — from classic Bikaneri Bhujia to smoky Banana Chips — we source the finest ingredients and honour traditional recipes that have stood the test of time.

Available exclusively at Reliance Retail stores across India, our snacks bring the authentic tastes of regional India to more than 1,000 stores nationwide.
